About NEST Cluster (Demo)

The North Eastern Science & Technology (NEST) Cluster is envisioned as a hub that connects academia, industry, and government agencies to catalyse innovation in the region. This demo page shows how information can be structured clearly for ministers, researchers, and citizens.
